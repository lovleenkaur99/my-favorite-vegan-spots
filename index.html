My Favorite Vegan Spots

Few of the best meatless options in New York City.

Beyond Sushi


Beyond Sushi

Beyond Sushi has some really delicious dishes. They offer variety of vegan sushi and other items in their menu. Their Fried Chick'n Bao is one of my pesonal Favorite dish. Their employees are quite helping and friendly.

HanGawi


HanGawi

HanGawi is a Korean vegan restaurant. Their atmosphere is one most different then any other restaurants, that I have experienced. They offer variety of traditional teas and dishes. If someone goes to the restaurant for the first or do not have enough knowledge about their food, the staff is more than welcome to ask fo recommendation. HanGawi is quite popular, so reservation is recommended.

Jajaja


Jajaja

Jajaja is a Mexican vegan restaurant. They offer mexican dishes with a vegan and modern twist. Their services are offered in four different locations in the city. It is great to go out at night and hangout.
